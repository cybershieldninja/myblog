The ISO 27000 series is a family of international standards designed to help organizations secure their information systematically. At its core, the series focuses on the Information Security Management System (ISMS), which is a structured framework for managing sensitive data, minimizing risks, and ensuring compliance.

Think of ISO 27000 as a toolbox for cybersecurity governance, helping IT professionals protect data, build trust, and maintain compliance.
